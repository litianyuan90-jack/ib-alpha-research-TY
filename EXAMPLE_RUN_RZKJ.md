# Example Run: 润泽科技（300442.SZ）

## Why the name matters
A-share scarce asset with exposure to:
- traditional IDC cash-flow base
- AIDC / intelligent computing center upgrade
- liquid-cooling delivery capability
- REIT-enabled asset monetization

## Key analytical warning
Do not value the company on headline PE alone if REIT-related disposal gains materially inflate net profit.

## Correct lens
- operating earnings lens
- cash earnings lens
- asset platform lens
- re-rating lens

## Practical conclusion
Treat it as a high-quality computing-infrastructure platform, not as a statistically cheap stock.
