# Diligence Checklist

## Primary-source pack
- Annual report
- Latest interim / quarterly report
- Exchange announcements
- Investor presentation / IR transcript
- Sector policy documents
- Main competitors' filings

## Numbers to normalize
- one-off disposal gain
- government subsidy
- fair-value change
- impairment reversal
- equity-method investment gain
- FX gain/loss

## Questions to force
- What is recurring?
- What is cyclical?
- What is narrative only?
- What is already priced in?
- What needs to happen for the stock to work?

## Computing-power sector drill-down
- capacity delivered
- utilization / rack on-shelf rate
- customer concentration
- power quota / PUE
- cooling architecture
- financing model
- asset monetization path
